THE TEAM

Meet the Secretariat

Meet our exceptional group of high school leaders who share a passion for diplomacy and global affairs. Originating from middle school MUN backgrounds, we've grown together as leaders, attending national conferences and proudly earning accolades along the way. Get to know the faces behind our dynamic camp experience, each bringing unique perspectives and expertise to empower the next generation of diplomats.

Classes

In the first four days of CAMPMUN, our leaders aim to help bolster delegate's Model UN skills and help them grow to their fullest extent. Through preparing comprehensive exercises and practices, we aim to prepare them for future competitions and public speaking in real world settings.

Conference

With the collaboration of volunteers from various high schools, the secretariat prepares the annual CAMPMUN conference, or CAMPMUNC III. CAMPMUNC is an opportunity for students to put practiced skills to the test while enjoying a fun and memorable experience with their peers!
